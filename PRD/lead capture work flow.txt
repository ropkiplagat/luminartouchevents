This Product Requirements Document (PRD) outlines an automated customer journey for an Australian-based events management company. The focus is on Phase 1: Lead Capture, Validation, and Initial Engagement.Product Requirements Document (PRD): Automated Event OnboardingVersion: 1.0Status: DraftTarget Region: Australia (Compliance: Privacy Act 1988)1. Executive SummaryThe goal is to replace manual lead handling with a seamless, automated "Enquiry-to-Validation" workflow. This system ensures that every lead is captured from Google, agrees to the company's Terms & Conditions (T&Cs) before submission, and receives instant multi-channel confirmation (Email & WhatsApp).2. User Flow & Customer JourneyEntry Point: Customer finds the business via Google (Search/Maps) and clicks the enquiry link.Validation Gate: Customer is directed to a dynamic form.Mandatory Review: The "Submit" button remains inactive until the user scrolls through or checks a box confirming they have read the T&Cs.Data Capture: Customer submits the form.Instant Engagement: * Email: Professional welcome message with a summary of their enquiry.WhatsApp: Immediate notification via the WhatsApp Business API.3. Functional Requirements3.1 Advanced Web Form (Lead Capture)T&C Interlock: The form must include a scrollable text area or a mandatory checkbox linked to the T&Cs. The logic must prevent form submission (Submit button state: Disabled) until this condition is met.Field Mapping: Capture Name, Phone (with AU prefix), Email, Event Type, Event Date, and Estimated Guest Count.Spam Protection: Integration of reCAPTCHA to prevent bot entries.3.2 Automated Communication EngineChannelTriggerContent RequirementsEmailForm SubmissionBranded header, "Thank You" message, copy of submitted details, and "What Happens Next" timeline.WhatsAppForm SubmissionShort, conversational greeting. Example: "Hi [Name], we've received your enquiry for [Event Date]! Our team will review the details and get back to you shortly."3.3 Payments & Booking (Future Phase)Integration with Stripe or Square for Australian-compliant AUD transactions.Automated invoice generation upon quote acceptance.4. Technical Stack RecommendationsTo ensure scalability and low maintenance, the following "No-Code/Low-Code" stack is recommended:Form Engine: Typeform or JotForm (Both support advanced "Conditional Logic" for the T&C submission gate).Automation Hub: Make.com (formerly Integromat) or Zapier to route data.Communication: SendGrid (Email) and Twilio/Wati (WhatsApp Business API).Database: Airtable or Google Sheets to log all enquiries for the sales team.5.
